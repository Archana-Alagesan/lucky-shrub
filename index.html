About Lucky Shrub

Lucky Shrub, based in Tucson, Arizona, delivers expert garden design, landscaping, and maintenance for residential, commercial, and industrial properties. This family-run firm runs a plant nursery to your one-stop shop to "transform any space into an oasis you can be proud of".

Jason and Maria founded the company from their shared love of plants: Jason leads as garden architect, while Maria oversees marketing and the nursery.
